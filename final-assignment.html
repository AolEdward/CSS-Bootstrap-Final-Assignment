What are Computer Processors?

A computer processor, also known as a CPU - Central Processing Unit, is the brain of the computer.

Performs operations on data and the programs. The main function of a processor is crunch numbers and produce an output

There are two major processor makers: Intel and Advance Micro Devices / AMD

About

A Processor or central processing unit is the brain of the computer. It is responsible for executing instructions and performing calculations
The CPU works by fetching instructions from memory, decoding them, and then executing the instructions.

The CPU does that billions of times each second and each core of the CPU can process and instruction in parallel.
